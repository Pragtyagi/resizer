How to Use

  1. Click "Upload Image" and select your image file (JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF).
  2. Choose your desired "Aspect Ratio". For specific dimensions, select "Custom Dimensions" and enter width and height.
  3. If not using "Custom Dimensions", enter your desired "Target Width". The height will adjust based on the selected aspect ratio.
  4. Select "Resize Mode":
    • Crop to Fit Aspect Ratio: The image will be cropped to perfectly match the target aspect ratio, then resized. No empty space will be visible.
    • Fit with Background (Letterbox): The entire image will be made to fit within the target aspect ratio. If the aspect ratios don't match, empty space will be filled with your chosen background color.
  5. Adjust the "Image Quality" slider (for JPEG/WebP formats) to balance file size and visual quality.
  6. Select your preferred "Output Format".
  7. The preview will update. Click "Download Resized Image".
